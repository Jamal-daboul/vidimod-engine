🎵  WILLIAM — BACKGROUND MUSIC FOLDER
=======================================

Drop your background-music files in THIS folder and they'll show up
automatically in the "Background music" picker on the Create Video page.

• Supported: .mp3, .m4a, .wav, .ogg
• Use royalty-free / licensed music only (YouTube can mute or claim copyrighted tracks).
• Good free sources: YouTube Audio Library, Pixabay Music, Free Music Archive, Uppbeat.
• Keep volume control in the app — you don't need to pre-lower the track here.
• Files are looped automatically if shorter than the video, and trimmed to length.

After adding files, click "Refresh" next to the music picker (or reload the page).
